How to add your own gesture

1. Select the folder where you want to save your new dataset and insert the gesture name.

2. Press Go!

3. Wait until the frame window is coming up and place your hand in the green square then press 'S' to start recording.

4. You should see a pop-up with the number of frames you recorded, the maximum is 3001, if you want to record less than that just press 'Q' when you're done.

5. Now let's train the network! Just select the dataset folder and then press Train! Based on your computer performance this may take a while.. so, go grab a snack and start your favorite Netflix series!
